# Privacy Policy for Forge

**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026

Forge is an open-source developer hub and environment configurator designed for mobile coders. We believe in transparency and respect your privacy.

## Data Collection and Usage
Forge does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, analytics, telemetry, or source code. 

All environment configurations, scripts, and guides execute entirely locally on your Android device within your Termux environment.

## Third-Party Services
While Forge itself does not collect data, utilizing the scripts provided in the hub may involve downloading standard developer packages (such as Node.js, Python, or Git) from their official package managers or repositories. These interactions are governed by the privacy policies of those respective maintainers.

## Open Source Transparency
Because Forge is 100% open-source, you can audit all shell scripts and codebase configurations directly in our GitHub repository before executing them on your device.

## Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about security or privacy, please open an issue in the [Forge GitHub Repository](https://github.com/brighto7700/forge/issues).
